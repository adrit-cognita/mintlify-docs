Mosaic Reporting is an internal radiology reporting platform built to streamline the creation, review, and distribution of diagnostic imaging reports. This documentation covers the full technical stack — from the React-based frontend and its subsystems, to the backend services and APIs, to the infrastructure supporting every environment.
